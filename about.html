About Me

Welcome to Artemis Grange - Where Florals Tell Stories

Adrienne Agostini Jones, Event Florist Extraordinaire

A Blooming Passion

Hello, and thank you for visiting Artemis Grange. I'm Adrienne, the heart and soul behind the blossoms that weave stories into life's most extraordinary moments. With a passion for flowers and an eye for elegance, I have dedicated my life to transforming events into unforgettable, fragrant dreamscapes. My journey as an event florist began four years ago, and it's been nothing short of a blossoming love affair. I find inspiration in the delicate petals of a single rose, the vibrancy of a peony, and the lush greenery that whispers the secrets of a love that's meant to last forever. Each event I create is a testament to my love for floral artistry and the unique stories that couples and families share.

Why Choose Artemis Grange

Unique Approach:

Small details matter! This is why I grow and source flowers of quality to match every event aesthetic.

Passion and Dedication:

Every event is different and unique. I am dedicated to designing florals to fit every unique event.

Expertise:

Customer Satisfaction:

My top priority is ensuring that every client's vision is brought to life and I go the extra mile to make every event extraordinary.
